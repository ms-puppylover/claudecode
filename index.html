Lesson 1 of 3

What AI Does Well

AI is an amazing tool when you use it the right way.
Let's learn how to make it your helper, not a shortcut.

👥 For Teens (11–18) 🕐 5–7 min read

Think in If–Then–Else Loops 🔄

Before you ask AI for help, run your decision through these loops.
They help you learn deeper, stay honest, and create your best work.

💡

The more intentional you are, the more AI helps you grow.

❓

Need Ideas to Get Started?

IF

I'm staring at a blank page and don't know where to begin.

↓
THEN

I'll ask AI for ideas, examples, or outlines to spark my thinking.

↓
ELSE

I'll try brainstorming first or looking at my class notes.

Example

IF I need a topic for my essay, THEN I'll ask AI for 5 unique angles, ELSE I'll free-write for 10 minutes.

💡

Need Help Understanding?

IF

I'm confused about a concept or question.

↓
THEN

I'll ask AI to explain it in simple words or give examples.

↓
ELSE

I'll reread my notes or ask a classmate/teacher.

Example

IF I don't get a math problem, THEN I'll ask AI for a step-by-step example, ELSE I'll try one similar problem first.

✏️

Writing a First Draft?

IF

I need help organizing my thoughts.

↓
THEN

I'll ask AI to create an outline or give me sentence starters.

↓
ELSE

I'll make a messy draft on my own first.

Example

IF I'm writing a history essay, THEN I'll ask AI for an outline, ELSE I'll jot down my main ideas first.

🔍

Stuck and Need Feedback?

IF

My draft feels off and I don't know why.

↓
THEN

I'll ask AI to give feedback, suggest improvements, or spot weak points.

↓
ELSE

I'll take a break and come back with fresh eyes.

Example

IF my paragraph sounds weak, THEN I'll ask AI how to make it stronger, ELSE I'll read it out loud to myself.

🛡️

Checking My Work (But Not Cheating)?

IF

I want to make sure my work is clear and correct.

↓
THEN

I'll ask AI to check grammar, clarity, or suggest edits without rewriting it.

↓
ELSE

I'll use my own checklist or ask a peer to review.

Example

IF I want better flow, THEN I'll ask AI to suggest transitions, ELSE I'll read it and highlight choppy spots.

    💬 THINK BEFORE YOU SHARE

    Pause. Check in with yourself.

    T

    True?

    Is it real or could it be AI?

    H

    Helpful?

    Will this help or harm someone?

    I

    Inspiring?

    Does it spread good vibes?

    N

    Necessary?

    Do I really need to share this?

    K

    Kind?

    Would I be proud if someone did this to me?

    🤔

    If you're not sure, don't post!

    Ask a trusted adult. 💚

    ✨ PROMPT LIKE A PRO

    Be clear, be specific, be you. 💡

    🎨 CREATE SOMETHING

    Use AI to start, then make it 100% yours.

    📝

    Writing

    Ask AI for a story starter, then write the rest yourself.

    🎨

    Art & Design

    Generate ideas for a poster, then create your own version.

    💡

    Projects

    Brainstorm with AI, but always add your unique ideas.

    🛡️ STAY SAFE & KIND

    Protect yourself and others online.

    🔒

    Keep it private

    Never share your personal info, address, or passwords with any AI tool.

    💜

    Be kind online

    Don't use AI to create fake images or spread rumours about others.

    🗣️

    Tell a trusted adult

    If something feels wrong or unsafe online, speak up. You're not alone.

    ⭐ THE GOLDEN RULES OF AI

    Simple rules, big impact.

    🎓

    Use AI to LEARN, not cheat.

    💡

    Add YOUR ideas. Make it original.

    💬

    Give credit when AI helps. (It's honest!)

    🧠

    Don't let AI do all the work. Your brain matters.
